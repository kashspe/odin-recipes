Cheese Soufflé

Prep Time
Cook Time
Difficulty
20 min
10 min
3

Description

A light and airy dish made from a béchamel base enriched with cheese, carefully folded with whipped egg whites to create a fluffy soufflé.

Ingredients

  • 50g butter
  • 50g flour
  • 300ml milk
  • 100g grated Gruyère cheese
  • 4 eggs
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Make the béchamel

    2. Melt butter in a saucepan. Add flour and stir for 2 minutes. Gradually pour in the milk, whisking to avoid lumps. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.

  2. Add the cheese

    3. Remove from heat and stir in the grated cheese. Let it cool slightly.

  3. Add the egg yolks

    4. Separate the egg whites from the yolks, and mix the yolks into the béchamel.

  4. Whip the egg whites

    5. Beat the egg whites until stiff, then gently fold them into the cheese mixture.

  5. Bake

    6. Pour the mixture into a buttered soufflé dish and bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 25-30 minutes. Don’t open the oven during baking.

  6. Serve immediately

    7. As the soufflé will deflate quickly.

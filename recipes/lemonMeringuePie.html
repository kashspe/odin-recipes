Lemon Meringue Pie

Prep Time
Cook Time
Difficulty
20 min
45 min
3

Description

A sweet and tangy pie filled with a fresh lemon curd and topped with a light and golden meringue.

Ingredients

  • Shortcrust pastry
    • 250g flour
    • 125g butter
    • 80g powdered sugar
    • 1 egg
    • A pinch of salt
  • Lemon curd
    • 3 lemons (juice and zest)
    • 150g sugar
    • 3 eggs
    • 1 tbsp cornstarch
    • 80g butter
  • Meringue
    • 3 egg whites
    • 150g sugar

Steps

  1. Make the pastry: Mix flour, salt, and butter until crumbly. Add the sugar and egg. Form a dough ball and refrigerate for 30 minutes. Roll out and precook the pastry for 15 minutes at 180째C (350째F).
  2. Prepare the lemon curd: In a saucepan, mix lemon juice, zest, sugar, cornstarch, and eggs. Cook over medium heat, stirring, until thickened. Stir in butter off the heat. Pour the curd over the baked pastry.
  3. Make the meringue: Beat the egg whites until stiff, gradually adding the sugar until glossy and firm. Spread or pipe the meringue over the lemon curd.
  4. Bake: Return the pie to the oven at 150째C (300째F) for 10-15 minutes until the meringue is golden.
  5. Cool before serving.

Classic Hamburger

Prep Time
Cook Time
Difficulty
20 min
10 min
1

Description

A juicy and flavorful burger with a grilled beef patty, topped with crispy lettuce, fresh tomatoes, and a tasty sauce, served in a soft brioche bun.

Ingredients

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 500g ground beef
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 tomato
  • A few lettuce leaves
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Salt, pepper
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • Oil for cooking

Steps

  1. Prepare the beef: Season the ground beef with salt and pepper, then shape it into 4 patties.
  2. Cook the patties: Heat a pan with some oil and cook the patties for 3-4 minutes on each side (adjust to your preferred doneness).
  3. Toast the buns: Slice the hamburger buns in half and lightly toast them.
  4. Assemble the burger: Spread mayonnaise on the bottom bun, add lettuce, tomato, the beef patty, cheddar slice, onion, and pickles. Top with ketchup and mustard, then place the top bun.
  5. Serve immediately with fries or a side salad.
